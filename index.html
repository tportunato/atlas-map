Signal Data Sources
GDP Growth Static
Last updated: Oct 2025
IMF World Economic Outlook (Oct 2025) — annual real GDP growth %. 2025 values are IMF forecasts published October 2025.
↗ IMF DataMapper: NGDP_RPCH
Required columns: country_iso2, year, value
API: REST · Free
Inflation (CPI) Static
Last updated: Dec 2025
Eurostat HICP — harmonised annual CPI %. 2025 full-year average derived from monthly Eurostat releases through Dec 2025.
↗ Eurostat: prc_hicp_aind
Required columns: country_iso2, year, value
API: REST · Free
Interest Rate Static
Last updated: Dec 2025
ECB / national central banks — 10yr government bond yield annual average %. Non-eurozone countries use own central bank rates.
↗ ECB Data Portal: FM dataset
Required columns: country_iso2, year, value
API: REST · Free
Economic Sentiment Static
Last updated: Dec 2025
European Commission ESI (Economic Sentiment Indicator) — composite of industry, consumer, construction and retail surveys. EU average = 100. Non-EU proxied from OECD consumer confidence.
↗ EC Business & Consumer Surveys
Required columns: country_iso2, year, value
API: REST · Free
Political Stability Static
Last updated: Sep 2025
World Bank Worldwide Governance Indicators (WGI) — Political Stability and Absence of Violence/Terrorism, rescaled 0–100. Updated annually.
↗ World Bank: WGI DataBank
Required columns: country_iso2, year, value
API: REST · Free
E-commerce Penetr. Static
Last updated: Nov 2025
Eurostat ICT Usage Survey — % of individuals who purchased goods/services online in past 12 months. Non-EU estimated from ECDB European E-Commerce Report 2024.
↗ Eurostat: E-commerce statistics
Required columns: country_iso2, year, value
API: REST · Free
